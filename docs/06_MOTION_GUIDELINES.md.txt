# MOTION GUIDELINES

## Purpose

Motion exists to support storytelling.

Animation should guide attention, create rhythm and reinforce the emotional experience.

Motion should never exist simply because it looks impressive.

---

# Overall Feeling

Motion should feel:

- Calm
- Cinematic
- Deliberate
- Confident
- Premium

Never:

- Chaotic
- Flashy
- Gimmicky
- Distracting

---

# Speed

Animations should generally feel slower than average.

Visitors should have time to appreciate imagery.

Avoid sudden movements.

---

# Scrolling

Scrolling should reveal content naturally.

The visitor should feel like they are discovering the portfolio.

---

# Hover Effects

Hover effects should be subtle.

Allowed:

- Small image zoom
- Slight brightness change
- Soft overlay transition

Avoid:

- Rotations
- Bouncing
- Large scaling
- Overly complex effects

---

# Page Transitions

Transitions between pages should feel seamless.

Whenever possible:

- Fade
- Gentle movement
- Smooth continuity

Avoid abrupt changes.

---

# Intro

The intro is the only highly choreographed animation.

It should feel like opening the portfolio rather than entering a website.

---

# Principle

Motion should never compete with the work.

It should quietly support it.
